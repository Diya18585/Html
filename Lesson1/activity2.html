What is Space?

When we look up at the sky at night, we see millions of stars and constellations, even planets sometimes! This is space.

Where does Space start?

Space starts at the Karman line which is 100 kilometers above sea level. It is where rockets have to fight the force of gravity to enter Space.

What is in Space?

Space is a vast, empty vacum consisting of Black holes, planets, stars,etc.
