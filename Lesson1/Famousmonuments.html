Famous Monuments from around the World

The Seven Wonders of The World

Seven Wonders of The World are seven monuments around the world that are famous for their beauty or history.

    • Great Wall of China

    The Great Wall of China is located in well...of course...China. The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built to protect the Chineese empire from invaders.

    • Christ The Redeemer

    A massive Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ located atop Corcovado Mountain in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Christ the Redeemer is a symbol of the city and a popular tourist attraction.

    • Taj Mahal

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal, and a symbol of love and architectural beauty.

    • The Colosseum

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site and an ancient Roman amphitheater in Rome, Italy, famous for hosting gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

    • Macchu Picchu

    An ancient Inca citadel nestled high in the Peruvian Andes, showcasing impressive stonework and offering a glimpse into the Inca civilization.

    • Chichén Itzá

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site and an ancient Mayan city in Mexico, known for its impressive pyramids, temples, and astronomical observatories.

    • Petra

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site and an ancient Nabataean city carved into sandstone cliffs in Jordan, featuring intricate facades and a rich history.
