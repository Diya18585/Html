Cuisines around the world

Different countries have different types of cuisines, each made from the native ingredients that grow there. Let's explore them!

Indian Food

Indian cuisine is renowned for its diverse flavors and spices, offering a wide range of vegetarian and meat dishes. It's characterized by the use of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili powder.

Italian Food

Italian food is known for its simplicity, fresh ingredients, and regional variations. It emphasizes pasta, pizza, and a variety of seafood and meat dishes, with a focus on fresh, high-quality, seasonal ingredients.

Mexican Food

Mexican cuisine is a vibrant blend of pre-Hispanic and Spanish culinary traditions, renowned for its diverse flavors and unique dishes. It's characterized by the use of corn, beans, chilies, and various regional ingredients, with Spanish influences adding meats, cheeses, and spices.

Meditternean cuisine

Mediterranean cuisine encompasses the diverse culinary traditions of countries surrounding the Mediterranean Sea, emphasizing fresh, wholesome ingredients like fruits, vegetables, olive oil, fish, and lean proteins.
